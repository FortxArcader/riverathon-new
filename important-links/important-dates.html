Timeline for Important Dates

June'25

Phase 1 : Ideation and Submission

Participants will brainstorm ideas and form teams.

August'25

Phase 2: Screening of Ideas & Assigning of Mentors

FEB' 25

Phase 3: Mentoring & Training Sessions

Phase 4: Development of Solutions

Teams will work on their prototypes, integrating LiDAR data with environmental solutions.

Continuous support from mentors in both technical (LiDAR, GIS) and ecological domains.

Nov'2025

Phase 5: Grand Finale and Announcement of Winner

Final solutions will be presented to a panel of judges.

The criteria for evaluation will include innovation, feasibility, impact on river restoration, and long-term sustainability.
